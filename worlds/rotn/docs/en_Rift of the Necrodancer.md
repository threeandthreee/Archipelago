# Rift of the Necrodancer

## What Does Randomization do to this Game?
- You will be given a number of starting songs. The number of which depends on your options.
- Completing any song will give you 2 rewards.
- The items you can recieve are Diamonds and Songs.  You may also recieve duplicate songs (depending on settings), healing and vibe power as filler.

## What is the Goal of Rift of the Necrodancer
The goal of Right of the Necrodancer is to collect a specified number of **Diamonds**.  Once you'vecollected enough Diamonds, the goal song will be unlocked.
The goal song can be checked in the top right corner where your connection info is.

## What is Required to Play the Game in Archipelago?
Only the base game is required to play.  DLC songs can be toggled in the options.  Note that any DLC songs released for free will be automatically included.

## Extra Info
- Regardless of what difficulties you select in the yaml, you can clear songs on any difficulty in game.
- Currently there is no way to check what songs you have or haven't done in game.  **Universal Tracker** is supported and recommended to have to help keep track.
